Investigation Overview¶

This project Investigates the influence of the parents background, test preparation etc on students performance

Dataset Overview¶

There are 1,000 students in the dataset with 8 features (gender,race/ethnicity,parental level of education,launch,test prepration course,math score,reading score,writing score, and an addtional created feature overall mean score
